## What It Does

Resume Matcher AI analyzes your resume against any job description and provides actionable insights to help you improve your ATS compatibility. Simply upload your resume (PDF) and paste the job description, and the app will:

- **Calculate your ATS Match Score** - Get an instant compatibility score showing how well your resume matches the job requirements
- **Identify Missing Keywords** - Discover which important skills and keywords from the job description are missing from your resume
- **Suggest Phrases** - Receive AI-powered suggestions for phrases you can add to better align with the job description
- **Provide Structure Feedback** - Get detailed feedback on your resume's structure, including section completeness, quantification usage, and formatting recommendations

## How It Works

The app uses advanced semantic similarity analysis combined with keyword matching to evaluate how well your resume aligns with a job description. It leverages:

- **Semantic Analysis** - Understands the meaning and context of your experience, not just exact keyword matches
- **Keyword Extraction** - Identifies important skills and terms from the job description
- **Structure Analysis** - Evaluates resume formatting, section presence, and quantification usage
- **AI-Powered Suggestions** - Uses large language models to provide intelligent recommendations
