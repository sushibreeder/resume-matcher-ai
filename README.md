## What It Does

Ever wonder if your resume will pass through those ATS systems? This app tells you:

- **Your ATS Match Score** - How well your resume matches the job (as a percentage)
- **Missing Keywords** - What important skills from the job description you're missing
- **Suggested Phrases** - AI-generated phrases you can add to your resume
- **Structure Feedback** - Tips on resume formatting, sections, and how to add more quantifiable achievements

## How to Use

1. Go to the [live app](https://klhkcsfzymylkndybhjpbh.streamlit.app/)
2. Upload your resume (PDF)
3. Paste the job description
4. Click "Analyze Resume"
5. Get instant feedback!

## Privacy

Your data is never stored. Everything is processed in your browser session and deleted immediately after analysis.

## Built With

- Streamlit
- Sentence Transformers (for understanding resume content)
